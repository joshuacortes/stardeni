Personalized Care for a Better Quality of Life

At Stardeni Assisted Living, we ensure every resident receives individualized care that supports physical, emotional, and social well-being. Our caregivers assist with daily tasks so residents can enjoy greater freedom and comfort.

We aim to provide a nurturing environment where seniors feel respected, supported, and truly at home — while family members feel confident knowing their loved one is receiving the highest quality of care.



What Our Assisted Living Provides

• Assistance with Activities of Daily Living (ADLs)
• Medication Assistance & Monitoring
• Nutritious Daily Meals & Snacks
• 24/7 Trained Caregiver Support
• Housekeeping & Laundry Services
• Social Activities & Companionship

Assisted Living Services

Daily Personal Care

Support with bathing, grooming, dressing, toileting, and mobility needs.

Medication Management

Scheduled reminders and assistance ensuring medications are taken correctly.

Healthy Meals & Snacks

Fresh, nutritious meals served daily with dietary needs in mind.

Safe, Comfortable Rooms

Fully furnished private or shared rooms designed for safety and comfort.

24/7 Support

Caregivers on-site around the clock for safety, supervision, and assistance.

Activities & Social Engagement

Daily opportunities for socializing, recreation, and emotional well-being.

Levels of Assisted Living Care

Level 1: Light Support

Ideal for residents who need occasional assistance with grooming, mobility, or reminders.

Level 2: Moderate Support

Daily assistance with multiple activities, medication support, and increased staff supervision.

Level 3: Enhanced Support

For residents requiring continuous assistance, safety oversight, and specialized care routines.
