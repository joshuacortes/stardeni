Helpful information

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between independent and assisted living?
Independent living focuses on social and daily living services for residents who are mostly independent. Assisted living provides additional personal care and medication support. Contact us for a personalized care assessment.
Do you offer meals for special diets?
Yes — we provide accommodations for special diets, food allergies, and texture-modified meals when needed.
How can I schedule a tour?
Call (951) 496-6577 or use the "Request a Tour" form on our contact page to schedule an in-person or virtual tour.
